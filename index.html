Digital Marketing Manager

Location: San Diego

About the job:

Doctible is a tech company, but what we do ultimately affects people's health and their wallets. We are looking for a stellar Digital Marketing Manager. You will take the reins and acquire new leads and customers through online PPC, CPA, content distribution, retargeting campaigns and whatever secret sauce you can bring to the table. This role will be in charge of implementing and scaling online acquisition marketing, managing the strategy, execution, and optimization across channels.

You understand the importance of building a portfolio of channels to maximize ROI and how to quickly turn the dials on them to hit company objectives. You're smart about testing and not afraid of trying new things, quickly interpreting the results and in the end, adding new channels to the mix the build the next big contributor to the program.

Other key characteristics would be: self-motivated, passionate and ready to prove themselves to become part of the leadership team and shape the company’s future. While we have a great team, we all wear multiple hats ... there will be times where you will need to be a designer, page builder, web engineer, form builder & content writer. You're hands on, make no excuses, and love it.

About You:

  • 2-3 (or more) years of experience in digital marketing (B2C).
  • In-depth knowledge of the various digital marketing channels and technologies, including paid search (Google AdWords), retargeting, social network advertising (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and more), and content distribution and placement networks.
  • Conversion expertise, with in depth knowledge of funnel tactics, and pushing aggressive tests through via Optimizely, Unbounce, or another equivalent.
  • Aptitude for online analysis, campaign development, campaign optimization and reporting campaign results. Read: you live in excel and love to dig into trends.
  • Prior experience in Healthcare or Health Insurance industry is beneficial, but not required.

To Apply:

Please send cover letter and your resume to careers@doctible.com. We'd love to learn more about you!

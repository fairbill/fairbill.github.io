Job Description

We are a customer-friendly online marketplace that allows consumers to shop procedures, view doctors, and get the best cash price. We're built on top of cutting edge technologies and continue to expand. We are looking for motivated, experienced Software engineer to work on a fast paced, collaborative team. You will be working across many exciting technologies including iOS, Ruby, AngularJS, Android and several data storage technologies. Somewhat flexible schedules since we're still pretty small. Plenty of room to grow and your opinions will be heard.

What are we looking in you?

  • A/BS degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience.
  • Demostration of excellent data-structure design and Object-Oriented design.
  • 3+ year experience of server-side work in Rails or other Rack-based frameworks
  • Good comfort level writing client/server side javascript code.
  • Familiarity with at least one of the relational databases.
  • Some level of understanding of server, hosting and networking.
  • Understanding and familiarity of building of REST API

You will be a rockstar if...

  • Movitated to learn new skills.
  • Habit of writing tests, code refactoring and code simplifying to achieve quality and efficiency.
  • Familiarity with one of the javascript frameworkes: EmberJS or AngularJS.
  • Excellent collaborative skills with outstanding verbal and written communication.
  • Willingness to work in an open, collaborative environment.
  • Familiar with HIPAA and have some background of working in Healthcare.

What we offer:

  • Competitive salary and equaity; plenty of room to grow.
  • Health plans and healthy working environment.
  • Working on a small collaborative team where your opinion matters.
  • Time to explore and experiement with new technologies.
